Support

Clarté  ·  Last updated April 2026

If something is not working or you have a question, here is how to get help.

Send feedback from the app

The fastest way to reach us is through the in-app feedback form. Open Clarté → Settings → About → Send feedback. Your message goes directly to the developer.

Common questions

  • My insight did not generate — Insight generation requires an internet connection and runs once a week. If it failed, Clarté will retry automatically on your next app open.
  • I want to delete all my data — Go to Settings → Your data → Delete all data. This permanently removes all entries, insights, and your anonymous identifier from the device.
  • Does Clarté back up my data? — No. All data is stored locally on your device and excluded from iCloud backup by design.
  • I forgot to log a previous day — Tap any day tile in the current week (Sunday to Saturday) on the Home screen to log or edit that day's entry. Days from previous weeks cannot be edited.
  • Can I change my notification time? — Yes. Go to Settings → Notifications → Daily reminder and adjust the time.

About your data

Your journal entries never leave your phone except to generate your weekly insight, which is processed anonymously and not stored. For full details, see the Privacy Policy.

Contact

For anything not covered above, use the in-app feedback form in Settings → About → Send feedback. We read every message.
