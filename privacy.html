Privacy Policy

Clarté  ·  Last updated April 2026

Clarté is designed to be private by default. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it goes, and what it does not do.

What stays on your device

All journal entries and mood data are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data is not synced to any server, not backed up to iCloud, and never leaves your device except as described below.

Anonymous identifier

On first launch, Clarté generates a random anonymous UUID and stores it in your device's Keychain. This identifier is not linked to your name, email address, Apple ID, or any other personal information.

AI insight generation

When generating a weekly or monthly insight, Clarté sends the following to a backend proxy server:

  • Your journal entries for the relevant period (mood and free-text fields)
  • Previous insight summaries for context

This data is forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your insight. The backend proxy does not store, log, or retain any of this data. Anthropic processes this data as a data processor on our behalf. For information on how Anthropic handles data, see anthropic.com/privacy.

Feedback

If you submit feedback via the in-app feedback form, the text of your feedback is sent to the backend proxy and forwarded to the developer by email. Feedback text is not logged or stored on the server. No personal information is attached to feedback submissions.

Data we do not collect

Clarté does not collect, store, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprinting data
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Crash reports sent to third-party services

Your right to delete your data

You can delete all data at any time from Settings → Your data → Delete all data. This removes all journal entries, insights, and the anonymous UUID from your device. The action is irreversible.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via the in-app feedback form in Settings → About → Send feedback.
